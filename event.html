As we are limited to only three category. Silver riders club are working as hard as possible to give the riders with more facilities and experience for the riders. It aims to organize cycling events in alternate route with various challenges.

Upcoming events to our clubs are mention below:

  • Short route cycling for fresher
  • More longer rides for our experienced riders
  • Social awarness cycling program against pollution, for charity
  • Coast to coast cycling stay for two days
  • Annual dinner to celebrate the success of the club during the year

Silver Riders Cycling club is not only providing opportunities and facilities but also aims to organize various events for refreshness as well as to increase the ability of the riders. Your ideas and views for the upgrade of our category is a must welcome. Share your views and ideas.
