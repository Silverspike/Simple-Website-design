We provide our riders with group category so our riders can be provided with their task and can ride with their desire. There are theree category you can choose:

1st Group

  • Starts at 9:15AM
  • More obstacles (more mountaineering area & offroads) for excitment
  • Covers average 50 miles
  • Recommended for adults

2nd Group

  • Starts at 9:30 AM
  • Less obstacles (less mountaineering & more plain area)
  • Covers average 35 miles
  • Recommended for childrens for 18+ age group

3rd Group

  • Starts at 10 AM
  • No obstacles
  • Covers average of 25 miles
  • Recommended for senior citizen and childrens

All groups meet up for lunch at a local pub where they can enjoy the beautiful view of the valley. Riders are also free to use the bath room of the pub and enjoy their stay.
